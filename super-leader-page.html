Mrs. Rachna Pant

Presenter Details

Mrs. Rachna Pant

Director at Ramjas School, RK Puram, New Delhi

Mrs. Rachna Pant has been an impassioned educator with a zeal for reformation and creation of an education system which is committed to creating an egalitarian society. She aims to develop an institute which will be a miniature of a world which is sensitive, equal and has ethos of compassion and acceptance.

She hails from a renowned academic family and is presently serving as Director in the prestigious Ramjas School, R.K. Puram, New Delhi.

She has won many prestigious and coveted awards, including the ‘President’s Award’, received at the hands of Hon’ble President of India, Dr. APJ. Abdul Kalam, for her lifelong devotion to the cause of education. In her career spanning across four decades, she had an enviable track record as a principal of outstanding calibre, educational expertise, author of repute, a woman of precision and an excellent administrator with high intellect,

She has donned many hats and won recognition as a CBSE Resource Person and an ELT Expert. She has found a mention and is acknowledged in credits of two very prestigious initiatives of CBSE, namely Art Integration and Experiential Learning. Her valued inputs were recognised and incorporated pan India.

As an author of more than 40 books, with focus on the less fortunate, first-generation village children from Uttar Pradesh, she has covered diverse topics like: language books, text books, value education and general knowledge books. To her credit is also an anthology of poems, titled, “Reflection” a reflection of her persona.

She was the founder Director Academic of the ‘Global Indian International School’, Bangkok. She started this 1st and only CBSE school in Thailand which had 3 boards i.e., CBSE, Cambridge and International Baccalaureate.

She is an International Consultant selected by the Govt. of India to train trainers of Uzbekistan. She has been conferred with the International School Award in Bangkok and received the Rex Karma Veer Award for Outstanding Leadership. Very recently, Zamit honoured her with Life Time Achievement Award.

The school has progressed exponentially under her leadership. Her futuristic vision and compassionate leadership skills have always motivated others. She has been instrumental in bringing to the school the prestigious Global Pilot projects such as HP-NETA (In collaboration with Hewlett Packard USA), MAST, Learnmeter (Australia -Teaching Maths and Science to middle school students through the Tablet Devices and Analysing the impact of technology-based learning, and developing intervention to overcoming the challenges), the installation of ATLto name a few.

She has also been awarded ‘Radhakrishnan Millennium National Teacher Award’ for her great contribution in the field of education, Yoga and Social Security and National Integration. According to her, there are no lifts or escalators to the route of success. There are only stairs leading from one level to the next. A step at a time.

Mrs. Rachna Pant is now also recognized by Zamit as a ‘Super Leader of India’.

Ms. Seetha Murthi

Director Education at Silver Oaks International Schools

The Gandhian mantra, You be the change you wish to see in the world, drives Seetha Murty to create develop and sustain progressive and generative learning environments. As the Director of Education of Silver Oaks International Schools in Hyderabad, Bangalore and Visakhapatnam, she assumes responsibility for the learning years of 6000 students in her schools.

3 decades of teaching experience in different geographies of India makes her a lifelong learner.

Besides leading IB workshops across the Asia Pacific region, she is also invited to present at 4 IB Global Conferences.

Research, curriculum development, teacher training are some of her strengths. She started a research centre within the school to develop curriculum based on cognitive leraning.

She has developed and published student centric books in Science, Math and English for students upto Grade8. And these are used within the 5 campuses she leads.

She developed books in Science, Math, English for Grades 1 to 8 which are published and used in Silver oaks International Schools.
