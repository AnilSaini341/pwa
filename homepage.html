Become a Super Leader

An exclusive programme designed especially for eminent leaders of the Zamit fraternity. This programme, to form a network of leaders determined to drive their schools towards future readiness, is by invitation only.

Apply Now
Lead by example and you will find yourself inspiring your peers, preparing your students for the future, sharing thoughts & knowledge at panels and building a global digital community.
Why you should apply

Recognition for yourself and your institution

Earn recognition on a national platform as a ‘Super Leader’ of your country that separates you from your peers and also help your institution achieve the ‘International School Award’ brought to you by Zamit.

Become an inspiring ‘Super’ in your community

Run Zamit Originals share views, ideas, thoughts and knowledge with your followers, connections, students and parents. Also be a part of the esteemed jury for Zamit Competitions

Grow your reputation as a thought leader

Get invited to panel discussions, talk shows, interviews or as keynote speakers and moderators
